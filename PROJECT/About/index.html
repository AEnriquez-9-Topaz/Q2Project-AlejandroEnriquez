About Filipino Bites


Welcome to Filipino Bites , the premier recipe-sharing website for Filipino cuisine! This is a Filipino-made company for our fellow Filipinos, hoping to spread the joy and pride of Filipino culture.


Our Services:
Here, you can check out various recipes that have been shared, and also share your own unique dishes!

Our Purpose:
We wish to showcase the rich Philippine culture to the world. We at Filipino Bites understand that, no matter what, Filipino food is what interconnects all Filipinos. It is a symbol of Filipino history and diversity, with everyone having different recipes that amalgamate into something much richer than the sum of its parts. We want this website to serve as a way for the Filipino community to connect through food. And, as a dream, we want this website to serve a purpose beyond the Philippines! We hope that this website will grow popular enough for the global community to see, so that they too can experience our amazing culture on their plate.


Whether you are a beginner or a chef, a Filipino or a foreigner, Filipino Bites is the best way to get the finest Filipino cuisine right onto your plate.
